# Privacy Policy  
**App Name:** Luggage Tag Together  
**Platform:** iOS  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-17  

## 1. Introduction  
Luggage Tag Together is designed with a privacy-first mindset. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your name to use the service. This policy explains how we manage the minimal data required to make the app function.

## 2. Information Collection & Privacy
- No Personal Accounts: We use anonymous authentication. We do not know who you are. A unique, randomized ID is assigned to your device locally to keep your tags and chats synced.

- App Users (Tag Owners): The iOS app does not access your device's GPS location. We only store your tag organization (Groups) and an anonymous push notification token so you can receive alerts.

- Scanners (Web Users): When someone scans your QR code, they have the optional choice to share their location. This data is provided voluntarily by the scanner via their web browser to help you identify where the scan happened.

## 3. Data Storage (Firebase)
All app data is hosted using Google Firebase.

- Data Types: We store your randomized User ID, your created tags/groups, and chat messages.

- Encryption: Data is stored in a non-encrypted format on our cloud backend to facilitate real-time features like the "Instant Notification" and "Tag Badges."

- Retention: Because accounts are anonymous, if you delete the app or clear your local data, your connection to these tags may be lost unless you have backed up your configuration.

## 4. How We Use Data
- Instant Alerts: To send a push notification to your device the moment a tag is scanned.

- Communication: To host the anonymous chat between you and the scanner.

- User Control: To power the "Block" feature, allowing you to silence specific scanners for a set time.

## 5. Your Rights 
- Deletion: You can delete any tag, group, or chat history directly in the app. This action purges the data from our Firebase database.

- Anonymity: You remain anonymous at all times. We do not track you across other apps or sell your data to third parties.
